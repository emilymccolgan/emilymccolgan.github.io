There are approximately 4000 different minerals, and each of those minerals has a unique set of physical properties. These include:

  1. color
  2. streak
  3. hardness
  4. luster
  5. specific gravity
  6. cleavage
  7. fracture

and many more.These physical properties are useful for identifying minerals. However, they are much more important in determining the potential industrial uses of the mineral.
